About MH-17

Our Passion, Your Pride

Founded in Sangamner, MH-17 Car Detailing Centre was born from a simple passion: a love for cars. We believe every vehicle, whether it's a daily driver or a weekend cruiser, deserves to look its absolute best. Our name, MH-17, is a tribute to our roots and our commitment to serving the local community with honesty and top-quality work.

Our team is a group of dedicated professionals who are trained in the latest detailing techniques. We use only the best products and equipment to ensure that your car receives a showroom-quality finish, every single time.

Our mission is to provide exceptional detailing services that protect your investment and restore your love for your car.
